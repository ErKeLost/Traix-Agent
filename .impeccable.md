## Design Context

### Users
This product is a Binance market terminal for active crypto traders and market researchers working in focused desk sessions. They use it to scan structure quickly, monitor live flow, validate trade ideas, and move between symbols without losing context.

### Brand Personality
Calm, professional, restrained.

The interface should feel precise and dependable rather than loud or speculative. It should evoke confidence, focus, and analytical control under pressure.

### Aesthetic Direction
Dark institutional trading desk.

Use a graphite and petroleum-blue base with muted brass highlights and disciplined semantic green/red for market state. Avoid gamer neon, glass-heavy UI, generic AI gradients, and flashy fintech tropes. The visual tone should feel like a high-end research terminal rather than a crypto landing page.

### Design Principles
1. Data first: hierarchy should make price, state, and controls immediately legible.
2. Controlled emphasis: use accent color sparingly for focus and selection, not decoration.
3. Dense but breathable: keep the interface efficient without becoming cramped.
4. Terminal-grade clarity: typography, spacing, and surfaces should communicate precision.
5. Motion with restraint: transitions should support state changes and never feel playful.
